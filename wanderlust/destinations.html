Best Travel Seasons

Plan your trip at the perfect time with our seasonal travel guide

Spring (March - May)

Perfect for: Europe, Japan, Mediterranean

  • Mild weather and blooming flowers
  • Fewer crowds than summer
  • Cherry blossoms in Japan

Summer (June - August)

Perfect for: Northern Europe, Canada, Alaska

  • Long daylight hours
  • Peak season for many destinations
  • Best weather for outdoor activities

Fall (September - November)

Perfect for: India, Nepal, Southeast Asia

  • Comfortable temperatures
  • Beautiful autumn colors
  • Post-monsoon clarity in Asia

Winter (December - February)

Perfect for: Southeast Asia, Australia, South America

  • Escape cold weather
  • Dry season in tropical regions
  • Summer in Southern Hemisphere
